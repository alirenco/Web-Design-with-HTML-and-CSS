Summary: Latitude vs. Wind Speed





It appears that the majority of the points are between the 0 mph and 10 mph. Wind speed data points seem to also be scattered fairly evenly resulting in no correlation between it and latitude. Wind speeds depend on the rise and fall of hot and cold air, respectively. There may not be as hot temperatures in January when these data were recorded. This makes sense since approximately half of the data points on the max temperature plot are between 50 and -50 degrees. Perhaps in a different season, the trends may be different?
